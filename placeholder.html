Mathematical Formulae

    1. \(\int \frac{dx}{x^2 + a^2}\)
    2. \(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^2 \pm a^2}}\)
    3. \(\int \frac{dx}{a^2 - x^2}\)
    4. \(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}}\)
    5. \(\int \frac{dx}{ax^2 + bx + c}\)
    6. \(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{ax^2 + bx + c}}\)
    7. \(\int \frac{(px + q)dx}{ax^2 + bx + c}\)
    8. \(\int \frac{(px + q)dx}{\sqrt{ax^2 + bx + c}}\)
    9. \(\int \sqrt{a^2 \pm x^2} \, dx\)
    10. \(\int \sqrt{x^2 - a^2} \, dx\)
    11. \(\dfrac{dy}{dx} + p(x)y = q(x)\)
