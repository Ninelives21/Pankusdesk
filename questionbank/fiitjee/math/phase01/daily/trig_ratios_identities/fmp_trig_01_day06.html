Given \(\tan \theta = \dfrac{b}{a}\), we have:

\(\sin \theta = \dfrac{b}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}, \quad \cos \theta = \dfrac{a}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}\)

\(\cos 2\theta = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta\)

\(= \dfrac{a^2}{a^2 + b^2} - \dfrac{b^2}{a^2 + b^2}\)

\(= \dfrac{a^2 - b^2}{a^2 + b^2}\)

\(\sin 2\theta = 2 \sin \theta \cos \theta\)

\(= \dfrac{2ab}{a^2 + b^2}\)

Therefore, \(a \cos 2\theta + b \sin 2\theta\) is:

\(a \left(\dfrac{a^2 - b^2}{a^2 + b^2}\right) + b \left(\dfrac{2ab}{a^2 + b^2}\right)\)

\(= \dfrac{a(a^2 - b^2) + b(2ab)}{a^2 + b^2}\)

\(= \dfrac{a^3 - ab^2 + 2ab^2}{a^2 + b^2}\)

\(= \dfrac{a^3 + ab^2}{a^2 + b^2}\)

\(= a\)

Proof for the case \( \dfrac{\cos A}{1 - \sin A} = \tan \left(45^\circ + \dfrac{A}{2}\right) \):

\(\begin{align*} &\text{First taking} \\ &\dfrac{\cos A}{1 - \sin A} = \tan \left(45^\circ + \dfrac{A}{2}\right) \\ &\text{RHS} = \dfrac{\tan 45^\circ + \tan \dfrac{A}{2}}{1 - \tan 45^\circ \tan \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{1 + \tan \dfrac{A}{2}}{1 - \tan \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &\text{LHS} = \dfrac{\cos A}{1 - \sin A} \times \dfrac{(1 + \sin A)}{(1 + \sin A)} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos A (1 + \sin A)}{1 - \sin^2 A} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos A (1 + \sin A)}{\cos^2 A} \\ &= \dfrac{1 + \sin A}{\cos A} \\ &= \dfrac{1 + 2 \sin \dfrac{A}{2} \cos \dfrac{A}{2}}{\cos^2 \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin^2 \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{\sin^2 \dfrac{A}{2} + \cos^2 \dfrac{A}{2} + 2 \sin \dfrac{A}{2} \cos \dfrac{A}{2}}{\cos^2 \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin^2 \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{(\cos \dfrac{A}{2} + \sin \dfrac{A}{2})^2}{(\cos \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin \dfrac{A}{2}) (\cos \dfrac{A}{2} + \sin \dfrac{A}{2})} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos \dfrac{A}{2} + \sin \dfrac{A}{2}}{\cos \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{1 + \tan \dfrac{A}{2}}{1 - \tan \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \text{RHS} \end{align*}\)

Proof for the case \( \dfrac{\cos A}{1 + \sin A} = \tan \left(45^\circ - \dfrac{A}{2}\right) \):

\(\begin{align*} &\text{RTP (required to prove)} \\ &\dfrac{\cos A}{1 + \sin A} = \tan \left(45^\circ - \dfrac{A}{2}\right) \\ &\text{RHS} = \dfrac{\tan 45^\circ - \tan \dfrac{A}{2}}{1 + \tan 45^\circ \tan \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{1 - \tan \dfrac{A}{2}}{1 + \tan \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &\text{LHS} = \dfrac{\cos A}{1 + \sin A} \times \dfrac{1 - \sin A}{1 - \sin A} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos A (1 - \sin A)}{1 - \sin^2 A} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos A (1 - \sin A)}{\cos^2 A} \\ &= \dfrac{1 - \sin A}{\cos A} \\ &= \dfrac{1 - 2 \sin \dfrac{A}{2} \cos \dfrac{A}{2}}{\cos^2 \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin^2 \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos^2 \dfrac{A}{2} + \sin^2 \dfrac{A}{2} - 2 \sin \dfrac{A}{2} \cos \dfrac{A}{2}}{\cos^2 \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin^2 \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{(\cos \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin \dfrac{A}{2})^2}{(\cos \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin \dfrac{A}{2}) (\cos \dfrac{A}{2} + \sin \dfrac{A}{2})} \\ &= \dfrac{\cos \dfrac{A}{2} - \sin \dfrac{A}{2}}{\cos \dfrac{A}{2} + \sin \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \dfrac{1 - \tan \dfrac{A}{2}}{1 + \tan \dfrac{A}{2}} \\ &= \text{RHS} \end{align*}\)
