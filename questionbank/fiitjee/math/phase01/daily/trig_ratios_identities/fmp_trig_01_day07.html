(i)
\( \sin A \cdot \sin (60^\circ + A) \cdot \sin (60^\circ - A) = \dfrac{1}{4} \sin 3A \)

LHS = \( \sin A \cdot \sin (60^\circ + A) \cdot \sin (60^\circ - A) \)

Using identity \( \sin (A + B) \cdot \sin (A - B) = \sin^2 A - \sin^2 B \),

LHS = \( \sin A (\sin^2 60^\circ - \sin^2 A) \)

= \( \sin A \left(\dfrac{3}{4} - \sin^2 A\right) \)

= \( \sin A \left(\dfrac{3 - 4 \sin^2 A}{4}\right) \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \left(3 \sin A - 4 \sin^3 A\right) \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \sin 3A \)

= RHS

(ii)
\( \cos A \cdot \cos (60^\circ + A) \cdot \cos (60^\circ - A) = \dfrac{1}{4} \cos 3A \)

LHS = \( \cos A \cdot \cos (60^\circ + A) \cdot \cos (60^\circ - A) \)

Using identity \( \cos (A + B) \cdot \cos (A - B) = \cos^2 A - \sin^2 B \),

LHS = \( \cos A (\cos^2 60^\circ - \sin^2 A) \)

= \( \cos A \left(\dfrac{1}{4} - \sin^2 A\right) \)

= \( \cos A \left(\dfrac{1 - 4 \sin^2 A}{4}\right) \)

= \( \cos A \left(\dfrac{4 \cos^2 A - 3}{4}\right) \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \cos A (4 \cos^2 A - 3) \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \cos 3A \)

= RHS

(iii)
\( \sin 20^\circ \cdot \sin 40^\circ \cdot \sin 60^\circ \cdot \sin 80^\circ = \dfrac{3}{16} \)

LHS = \( \sin 20^\circ \cdot \sin 40^\circ \cdot \sin 60^\circ \cdot \sin 80^\circ \)

= \( \sin 20^\circ \cdot \sin (60^\circ - 20^\circ) \cdot \sin 60^\circ \cdot \sin (60^\circ + 20^\circ) \)

= \( \sin 20^\circ \cdot \sin (60^\circ + 20^\circ) \cdot \sin (60^\circ - 20^\circ) \cdot \sin 60^\circ \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \sin 60^\circ \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \) (from the above proof)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \)

= \( \dfrac{3}{16} \)

= RHS

(iv)
\( \cos \dfrac{\pi}{9} \cdot \cos \dfrac{2\pi}{9} \cdot \cos \dfrac{3\pi}{9} \cdot \cos \dfrac{4\pi}{9} = \dfrac{1}{16} \)

LHS = \( \cos \dfrac{\pi}{9} \cdot \cos \left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} - \dfrac{\pi}{9}\right) \cdot \cos \dfrac{3\pi}{9} \cdot \cos \left(\dfrac{\pi}{3} + \dfrac{\pi}{9}\right) \)

= \( \cos \dfrac{\pi}{9} \cdot \cos \left(60^\circ - \dfrac{\pi}{9}\right) \cdot \cos \dfrac{\pi}{3} \cdot \cos \left(60^\circ + \dfrac{\pi}{9}\right) \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \cos \left(3 \times \dfrac{\pi}{9}\right) \cdot \cos \dfrac{\pi}{3} \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \cos \dfrac{\pi}{3} \cdot \cos \dfrac{\pi}{3} \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{2} \)

= \( \dfrac{1}{16} \)

= RHS
