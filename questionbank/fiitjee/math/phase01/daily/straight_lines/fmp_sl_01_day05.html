  1. Identify the Equations of the Sides:
    • \( 7x + y - 10 = 0 \)
    • \( x - 2y + 5 = 0 \)
    • \( x + y + 2 = 0 \)
  2. Find the Vertices of the Triangle:
    • \( A(1, 3), B(-3, 1), C(2, -4) \)
  3. Find the Altitude from \( A(1,3) \) to BC \( (x + y + 2 = 0) \):
    • The slope of BC is \( -1 \), so the slope of the altitude from \( A \) is \( 1 \).
    • Given one point is A(1, 3)
    • Subtitute in y = mx + c to get c = 2
    • Equation: \( y = x + 2 \)
  4. Find the Altitude from \( B(-3, 1) \) to AC \( 7x + y - 10 = 0
    • The slope of AC is \( -7 \), so the slope of the altitude from \( B \) is \( \dfrac{1}{7} \).
    • Given one point is B(-3, 1)
    • Subtitute in y = mx + c to get c = \( \dfrac{10}{7} \)
    • Equation: \( 7y = x + 10 \)
  5. Find the Intersection of the Altitudes:
    • Solve \( y = x + 2 \) and \( 7y = x + 10 \):
    • \( \left( -\dfrac{2}{3}, \dfrac{4}{3} \right) \)

The orthocenter is \( \left( -\dfrac{2}{3}, \dfrac{4}{3} \right) \).


  1. Identify the equations of the sides:
    • AB : \(x + 2y - 4 = 0\)
    • BC : \(5x + 3y + 1 = 0\)
    • AC : \(3x - y - 5 = 0\)
  2. Find the vertices of the triangle by solving the intersection points:
    • \(A = \text{Intersection of } AB \text{ and } AC = (2, 1)\)
    • \(B = \text{Intersection of } AB \text{ and } BC = (-2, 3)\)
    • \(C = \text{Intersection of } BC \text{ and } AC = (1, -2)\)
  3. Let the circumcenter be \(D(x, y)\):
    • We need to solve \(AD^2 = BD^2 = CD^2\).
  4. From \(AD^2 = CD^2\):
    • Equation: \(x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 2y + 5 = x^2 + y^2 - 2x + 4y + 5\)
    • Simplifying, we get \(x = -3y\).
  5. From \(AD^2 = BD^2\):
    • Equation: \(x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 2y + 5 = x^2 + y^2 + 4x - 6y + 13\)
    • Simplifying, we get \(-2x + y = 2\).
    • Substituting \(x = -3y\), we get \(y = \dfrac{2}{7}\) and \(x = -\dfrac{6}{7}\).

Therefore, the circumcenter is \(\left( -\dfrac{6}{7}, \dfrac{2}{7} \right)\).

  4. Use the formula for incenter coordinates:
    • \(I = \left( \dfrac{ax_1 + bx_2 + cx_3}{a + b + c}, \dfrac{ay_1 + by_2 + cy_3}{a + b + c} \right)\)
    • Substituting the values:
      • \(\dfrac{\sqrt{2} + 3\sqrt{10} + 6\sqrt{2}}{3\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{10}}\)
