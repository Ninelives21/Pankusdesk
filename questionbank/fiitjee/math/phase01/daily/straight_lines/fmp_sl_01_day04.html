1. Calculate the length of \( BC \):

\( BC = \sqrt{(-4 - (-2))^2 + (6 - (-5))^2} = \sqrt{4 + 121} = \sqrt{125} \)

2. Calculate the length of \( AB \):

\( AB = \sqrt{(2 - (-2))^2 + (3 - (-5))^2} = \sqrt{16 + 64} = \sqrt{80} = 4\sqrt{5} \)

3. Calculate the length of \( AC \):

\( AC = \sqrt{(2 - (-4))^2 + (3 - 6))^2} = \sqrt{36 + 9} = \sqrt{45} = 3\sqrt{5} \)

4. Use the section formula to find \( P \) which divides \( BC \) in the ratio of \( AB:AC \) or \( 4:3 \):

\( P = \left( \dfrac{4(-4) + 3(-2)}{4+3}, \dfrac{4(6) + 3(-5)}{4+3} \right) = \left( \dfrac{-22}{7}, \dfrac{9}{7} \right) \)

Therefore, the correct answer is choice (A).
