\( x_1 = 1, y_1 = 3 \)
\( x_2 = 2, y_2 = 7 \)

\( P = \left( \dfrac{mx_2 + nx_1}{m+n}, \dfrac{my_2 + ny_1}{m+n} \right) \)

\( = \left( \dfrac{3 \cdot 2 + 4 \cdot 1}{3 + 4}, \dfrac{3 \cdot 7 + 4 \cdot 3}{3 + 4} \right) \)

\( = \left( \dfrac{6 + 4}{7}, \dfrac{21 + 12}{7} \right) \)

\( = \left( \dfrac{10}{7}, \dfrac{33}{7} \right) \)

To find the coordinates of the points that divide the line joining \((-1, 2)\) and \((4, 5)\) in the ratio \(2 : 3\) internally and externally, we use the section formula:

For internal division:

\[ \text{P}_{\text{int}} = \left( \dfrac{mx_2 + nx_1}{m + n}, \dfrac{my_2 + ny_1}{m + n} \right) \]

Substituting \(m = 2\), \(n = 3\), \(x_1 = -1\), \(y_1 = 2\), \(x_2 = 4\), and \(y_2 = 5\):

\[ \text{P}_{\text{int}} = \left( \dfrac{2 \cdot 4 + 3 \cdot (-1)}{2 + 3}, \dfrac{2 \cdot 5 + 3 \cdot 2}{2 + 3} \right) = \left( \dfrac{8 - 3}{5}, \dfrac{10 + 6}{5} \right) = \left( 1, \dfrac{16}{5} \right) \]

For external division:

\[ \text{P}_{\text{ext}} = \left( \dfrac{mx_2 - nx_1}{m - n}, \dfrac{my_2 - ny_1}{m - n} \right) \]

Substituting \(m = 2\), \(n = 3\), \(x_1 = -1\), \(y_1 = 2\), \(x_2 = 4\), and \(y_2 = 5\):

\[ \text{P}_{\text{ext}} = \left( \dfrac{2 \cdot 4 - 3 \cdot (-1)}{2 - 3}, \dfrac{2 \cdot 5 - 3 \cdot 2}{2 - 3} \right) = \left( \dfrac{8 + 3}{-1}, \dfrac{10 - 6}{-1} \right) = \left( -11, -4 \right) \]

First point:

\(m = 1, n = 2\)

\(x_1 = 1, x_2 = -3\)

\(y_1 = -2, y_2 = 4\)

\(P_1 = \left( \dfrac{m x_2 + n x_1}{m+n}, \dfrac{m y_2 + n y_1}{m+n} \right)\)

\(P_1 = \left( \dfrac{1 \cdot (-3) + 2 \cdot 1}{1+2}, \dfrac{1 \cdot 4 + 2 \cdot (-2)}{1+2} \right)\)

\(P_1 = \left( \dfrac{-3 + 2}{3}, \dfrac{4 - 4}{3} \right)\)

\(P_1 = \left( \dfrac{-1}{3}, 0 \right)\)

Second point:

\(m = 2, n = 1\)

\(x_1 = 1, x_2 = -3\)

\(y_1 = -2, y_2 = 4\)

\(P_2 = \left( \dfrac{m x_2 + n x_1}{m+n}, \dfrac{m y_2 + n y_1}{m+n} \right)\)

\(P_2 = \left( \dfrac{2 \cdot (-3) + 1 \cdot 1}{2+1}, \dfrac{2 \cdot 4 + 1 \cdot (-2)}{2+1} \right)\)

\(P_2 = \left( \dfrac{-6 + 1}{3}, \dfrac{8 - 2}{3} \right)\)

\(P_2 = \left( \dfrac{-5}{3}, \dfrac{6}{3} \right)\)

\(P_2 = \left( \dfrac{-5}{3}, 2 \right)\)
