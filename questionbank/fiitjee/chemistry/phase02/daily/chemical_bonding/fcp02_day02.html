Correct Answer: (B) NaCl < LiCl < BeCl2

Explanation: The covalent character increases with the cation's charge density and polarizing power. Na⁺ is the largest cation with the lowest charge density, so NaCl has the least covalent character. Li⁺ is smaller and more polarizing, making LiCl have more covalent character than NaCl. Be²⁺ is the smallest and most highly charged cation, making BeCl₂ the most covalent.

In \( N_2O_5 \), one of the nitrogen-oxygen bonds is a coordinate covalent bond, where nitrogen shares a lone pair with oxygen.
