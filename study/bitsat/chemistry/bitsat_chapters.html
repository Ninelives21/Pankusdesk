    11. Second Law of Thermodynamics - Page C44
      1. Entropy S: measure of randomness or disorder
      2. Delta S = S_f - S_i
      3. Delta S = S_products - S_reactants
      4. Delta S = Delta H / T
      5. Spontaneous process favours increase in randomness
      6. Delta S_fusion = S_liquid - S_solid
      7. Delta S_vaporization = S_vapour - S_liquid
      8. Delta S_sublimation = S_vapour - S_solid
      9. Reversible equilibrium process: total entropy remains constant
      10. Irreversible process: total entropy increases
      11. Delta S_total > 0: spontaneous
      12. Delta S_total < 0: non-spontaneous
      13. Delta S_total = 0: equilibrium
