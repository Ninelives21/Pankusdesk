SECTION : B

  1. Short Stories Passage (Reading Comprehension) : 4 out of 6 → 4 marks

SECTION : C

  1. Punctuations : 8 out of 10 → 4 marks

  4. Verb pattern : 4 out of 4 → 4 marks
  5. Filling in Forms : 8 out of 10 → 4 marks
  6. Curriculum Vitae : 1 – no choice → 4 marks
  7. Letter Writing : 1 out of 2 → 4 marks
  8. Note making : 1 – no choice → 4 marks
  9. Stress – Marking : 8 out of 10 → 4 marks
  10. Dialogue making : 1 out of 2 → 4 marks
