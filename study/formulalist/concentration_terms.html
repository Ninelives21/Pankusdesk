1. Molarity: Number of moles of solute in 1L of solution (M)

      \[ \text{Molarity} = \dfrac{\text{moles of B}}{\text{Volume of solution (L)}} \]

      \[ = \dfrac{W_B}{M_B} \times \dfrac{1000}{V(\text{mL})} \]

      where:

      • \( W_B \): Given mass
      • \( M_B \): Molar mass of solute

      Dilution Formula

      \[ M_1 V_1 = M_2 V_2 = \text{constant} \]

      Molarity of a Mixture

      \[ M = \dfrac{M_1 V_1 + M_2 V_2}{V} \]

      where \( V = V_1 + V_2 \)
