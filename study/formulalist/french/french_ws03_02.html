Normandy: Bordered by the English Channel, Normandy has a rich history. It is particularly known today for the landing of Allied troops during World War II.
Normandy is famous for Camembert cheese, and the regional drink is cider. Caen and Rouen are two important cities with many universities.
Trouvaille and Deauville are seaside resorts, and Le Havre is an important port in this region. Many young people visit this region during their summer camps.
Mont Saint Michel is the most famous tourist site in this region.
