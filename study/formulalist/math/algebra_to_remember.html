ALGEBRAIC IDENTITIES

\(= x^2 + 2xy + y^2\)
\(= x^2 - 2xy + y^2\)
\(= (x - y)(x + y)\)
\(= x^2 + x(a + b) + ab\)
\(= x^2 + y^2 + z^2 + 2xy + 2yz + 2zx\)
\(= x^3 + y^3 + 3xy(x + y)\)
\(= x^3 - y^3 - 3xy(x - y)\)
\(= (x + y + z)(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 - xy - yz - zx)\)
\(= (x + y)(x^2 - xy + y^2)\)
\(= (x - y)(x^2 + xy + y^2)\)
