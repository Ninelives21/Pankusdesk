  • Naming of Polyfunctional Compounds (More than One Functional Group)

    1. Introduction
      1. There can only be one primary functional group in a compound.
      2. The primary functional group is as per a pre-defined priority order, and the remaining are to be considered substituents.

      3. Mnemonic for priority order and suffix naming
        "Car S An Tro (aisi thi) jaise Ambassador CIAKA Tempo (thi)."
        Serial No Mnemonic Group FG Suffix Substituent Prefix TFG when attached directly to C-ring
        1 Car Oic acid Carboxy Carboxylic Acid
        2 S Sulphonic acid Sulpho X
        3 An Oic anhydride X X
        4 Tro Alkyl Alkanoate Alkoxy Carbonyl Alkyl Carboxylate
