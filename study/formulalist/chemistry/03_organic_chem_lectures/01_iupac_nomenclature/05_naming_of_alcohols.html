Naming Of Alcohols

    1. Alhohols have an "-OH" group attached to the Carbon atom of an alkyl group.
    2. IUPAC Secondary suffix = "ol".
    3. Start numbering of the Carbon chain in a way that the Functional Group get sthe lowest locant/number.


  2. Examples on IUPAC and Common Names
    1. Common name = "Alkyl Alcohol".
    2. Common name of Methanol is Methyl Alcohol.
    3. Common name of Ethanol is Ethyl Alcohol.
