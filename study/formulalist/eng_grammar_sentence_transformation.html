ACTIVE PASSIVE VOICE

  1. Sufficient water is taken in a kettle.
  2. Tea powder is added.
  3. It is put on the stove.
  4. Milk is added.
  5. It is stirred two or three times.
  6. The hot tea is strained.
  7. Sugar is added.
  8. It is served in eco-friendly cups.
  • Yoga is practiced by us every day in the morning.
  • All the arrangements will be made by him.
  • The verdict was declared by the judge.
  • The results had already been announced before we entered the hall.
  • Their precious lives were sacrificed by many students for the sake of Telangana.
  • Some books were borrowed from the library by the students.
  • He cannot be saved by anybody.
  • How much loan amount has been sanctioned by the Bank?
  • The helmet should be worn while riding a two-wheeler.
  • All problems cannot be solved by money alone.
  • Let the lights be switched off.
  • Let silence be maintained in the prayer hall.
  • Many formalities have to be undergone for getting a visa.
  • The strike was called off by the workers.
  • The lesson is being explained by the teacher.
  • The courier agents will deliver the parcels at any time.
  • Chandra invited Surya to tea.
  • Traffic rules must be followed.
  • We wash vegetables before cooking.
  • How many times did I remind you of the medicine?
  • Keep the dustbin away from the eatables.
  • Did you invite all your friends to your birthday?
  • One cannot change every sentence into the passive voice.
  • If you don’t apply the ointment to the wound, it will not heal.
  • No one in his school has ever beaten my brother at chess.
  • The villagers believe that Sammakka and Saralamma are the saviors of their lives in times of crisis.

  • A TV set is being bought by him at the moment.
  • 100 saplings have been planted by me so far.
  • The newspaper was being read by them.
  • It had already been answered by her.
  • An essay will be written by me tonight.
  • It will have been posted by you by Monday.
  • Can the violin be played by her?
  • It may not be telecast by them.
  • One’s duty must be done.
  • Let the doctor be called in.
  • Let the door be closed.
  • It has to be done by the Government.
  • My vote has already been cast by someone.
  • By whom could he be helped?
  • We saw him crossing the road.
  • We advise you to be careful.
  • Let me see the picture.
  • I paid for her purchases.
  • There are no shops to let (out).
  • A mistake has been made by me.
  • You will be respected by your students a great deal more for your frankness and honesty.
  • Let the attention of your near neighbour at the table be called to the excellence of coffee.
  • Is Pythagoras Theorem or Newton’s Law of Gravity applied by you?

  • Trees were planted, fenced, watered and guarded by her.
  • I was given greater strength by their hope and encouragement. (OR) Greater strength was given to me by their hope and encouragement.
  • Let that hatter be removed instantly.
