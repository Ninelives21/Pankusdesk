  • Bohr's Atomic Model

    1. Atom has a centre called Nucleus.
    2. Electron revolves only in fixed circular orbits with fixed energy and fixed velocity.
    3. Quantisation Condition: Electrons revolve only in those circular orbits for which the angular momentum (\(L\)) is an integral multiple of \(\dfrac{h}{2\pi}\).
