CHEMISTRY IN EVERYDAY LIFE

No. Term Definition / Information Examples
1 Artificial sweetening agents These are low calorie sweeteners used as sugar substitutes. \(\mathrm{Saccharin,\ Aspartame,\ Sucralose}\)
2 Food preservatives These are chemical substances used to protect food against bacteria, yeasts and moulds. \(\mathrm{Table\ salt,\ Sugar\ syrup}\)
3 Antacids These are drugs used to remove excess acid in the stomach and maintain normal pH. \(\mathrm{Omeprazole,\ Lansoprazole}\)
4 Antibiotics These are drugs used to inhibit the growth of or destroy micro-organisms and are produced by micro-organisms. \(\mathrm{Penicillin,\ Chloramphenicol,\ Sulphadiazine}\)
5 Antiseptics These are drugs used to kill or prevent the growth of micro-organisms. \(\mathrm{Dettol,\ Bithional,\ Tincture\ of\ iodine}\)
6 Antihistamines These are drugs used to diminish the effect of histamine and prevent allergy. \(\mathrm{Dimetane\ (Dimetapp),\ Terfenadine\ (Seldane)}\)
7 Analgesics These are drugs used to reduce or abolish pain without causing disturbances to the nervous system. \(\mathrm{Morphine,\ Codeine,\ Aspirin,\ Ibuprofen}\)
8 Tranquilizers These are drugs mainly used in the treatment of psychoses and neuroses. \(\mathrm{Luminal,\ Seconal,\ Barbituric\ acid}\)
